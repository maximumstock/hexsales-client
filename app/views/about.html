FAQ

What should I use hexsales.net for?

The primary concern of this website is to document and visualize what happens within the auction house economy of HEX. That means that my first priority is generating concise but meaningful reports of certain time slots.

Where do you get your data from?

The data is provided by HexEnt and can be found here.

Why don't you calculate daily differences like HexPrice does?

I think that there is simply not enough traffic to make qualified statements about the state of the market for certain articles. For each rarity group a vast majority of sales for most articles happen within one of the two currency markets. For example, most Commons are sold for Gold, while most Legendaries are sold for Platinum. Most articles are't even sold regularly enough to check their sales in their respective main currency markets. I also don't think that going for percentage differences in prices is valid at the moment. I think you have to include multiple parameters to make meaningful statements about the current market situation for a certain article. However, I think that this is simply not possible at the moment, due to the lack of traffic. We'll see what happens once the HEX starts to receive some more attention through Steam, etc.
